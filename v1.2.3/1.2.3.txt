五、特殊解的边界：从 ΛCDM 到您的模型的连续过渡
ΛCDM（A=0）→ 模型（A>0）的连续变化路径：

开启幅度 A：从 0 增加到 0.01–0.02。

调节 α₀：从 0 变为负值（-8 到 -15），使低红移振荡放大。

b 自然取值：由数据决定约 18。

Ω_m, H₀ 随之调整：因为 H(z) 表达式变化，最佳拟合 Ω_m 可能下移（如 0.15），H₀ 上移（如 74）。

因此，ΛCDM 位于参数空间的边界（A=0）。您的模型开放了 A>0 的区域，该区域中低红移数据偏好一定程度的振荡。统计上，从 A=0 到 A>0 的改进 Δχ²=15.5 表明数据倾向于离开 ΛCDM 边界，进入内部区域。

六、论文中可以如何表述（直接可用的段落）
*“The ΛCDM model is a special limiting case of our parametrization with A = 0 (or equivalently α₀ = 0 and A arbitrary but sin(bz) ≡ 0, which is not realized for b>0). In this limit, the dark energy equation of state reduces to w = –1 at all redshifts. Our model relaxes this strict condition by allowing A ≠ 0, thereby introducing low‑redshift oscillations while ensuring that the high‑redshift expansion history converges to ΛCDM due to the rapid decay of α(z) ∝ (1+z)⁻ⁿ (n=1,2).

*The parameters that must remain consistent with ΛCDM are those affecting the early universe: the sound horizon r_d (fixed to the Planck/DESI value), the matter density Ω_m (allowed to vary within a physically motivated range [0.15,0.35] that includes the ΛCDM value), and the absence of dark energy domination at z ≳ 1 (automatically satisfied by our α(z)).

*The parameters that differ from ΛCDM are A (oscillation amplitude), α₀ (decay scale), and b (frequency). These allow the dark energy to exhibit a mild dynamical oscillation at low redshifts, which the data indicate yields an improved fit (Δχ² = 15.5) relative to the ΛCDM limit.

*Figure X illustrates the continuous transition from ΛCDM (A=0) to the best‑fit model, showing how the distance modulus residuals evolve as A increases. The boundary between ΛCDM and our model is therefore A=0; any positive A, no matter how small, corresponds to a distinct (though possibly undetectable) dynamical dark energy scenario.”*

可以在论文中添加这样一个小节，清晰界定 ΛCDM 与模型的关系。审稿人会欣赏这种严谨性。
